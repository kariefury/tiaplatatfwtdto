What is a hardware software interface?

That can be a tricky question. A simple answer is it is the waveforms that describe how the data will be sent from the hardware (like a microphone sensor) into the software (like a website). Many different things are covered by that simple definition and it might seem like they are unrelated, but they are all hardware software interfaces.
